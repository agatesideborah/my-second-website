treatment for illness

Treatment for illness can vary greatly depending on the specific disease, but generally involves a combination of approaches, including rest, hydration, symptom relief, and in some cases, medication.

Basic laboratory



Basic laboratory tests are routine medical tests that assess overall health and help diagnose or monitor various conditions. Common tests include a complete blood count (CBC), blood chemistry panel (BMP or CMP), lipid profile, thyroid function tests, and urinalysis.
